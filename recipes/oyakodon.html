Oyakodon Recipe

Description:

Oyakodon is a delicious Japanese meal consisting of chicken meat and egg. The name comes from "Oya (親)" and "Ko(子)", which mean "parent" and "child" respectively. Although it is a humorous and even slightly morbid joke, it does not take away from the deliciousness of this meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups of dashi
  • ¼ cup soy sauce (you can put as much soy sauce as you want imo)
  • 3 tablespoons of mirin
  • 4 large eggs
  • Rice (amount is to your discretion)

Steps:

  1. Cook Oyakodon
  2. Eat Oyakodon
